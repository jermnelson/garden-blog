# Snow in the Garden
This past week we had three days of snow which deposited at least six 
inches. This shouldn't be much of a surprise as Bayfield is at 6,900 feet in
elevation in the foothills of the San Juan Mountains in Southern Colorado. 

Yesterday and today the sun is melting some of the snow but you can still see
the garden beds and compost pile covered with snow:
