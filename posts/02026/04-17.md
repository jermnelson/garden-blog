# April Freeze

went below freezing (32&#176;F/0&#176;C) and unfortunately both
apple trees were in full bloom. Today when I went to check on the
trees, I did notice that many of the earlier blossoms where now
dried out or desiccated but I also had some hope because I noticed
new blossoms were emerging from some of the branches as seen below:
