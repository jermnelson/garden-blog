# Southwest Garden Bed Prep
I just finished the book, [The Backyard Homestead](https://id.loc.gov/resources/works/15587552.html)
by Carleen Madigan, and in early chapter, she recommends a method for preparing
your raised garden beds. Following her suggestions on the Southwest Garden Bed, 
I dug down about a shovel depth and transferred the entire first row to my Gorilla
wheel-barrow. Then I used about 1/2 of a bag of mushroom compost and used my new
pitch fork to mix up the bottom of the trench. For the next trench, I moved the 
dirt into the first trench, composted and the mixed the bottom, and then moved to
the third trench, shoveling the dirt to the previous trench. For the last trench
(there were only 4) I used the dirt I had saved in the wheelbarrow.
