# Three Sisters Planting and Soon Apple Blossoms
Yesterday, I purchased three seeds to get started on the three sisters planting
in the Southwest garden bed. Here are the seeds I purchased:

![Pumpkin, Green Bean, and Sweet Corn Seeds](img/02026-03-29-pumpkin-corn-bean-seeds.png)

- Pumpkin Sugar Pie *Cucurbata pepo*
- Corn Sweet Buttergold *Zea mayas* (hybrid)
- Bean Pole Blue Lake *Phaseolus vulgaris*

My **Three Sisters** bed this year is the Southwest bed and I prepared two 
raised planting areas. The first is in the Northeast circle that I planted 
multiple corn intermixed with the green bean seeds here (with a picture of the
initial pH analog). I then planted three of the pumpkin seeds in the three corners
of the circle.

![First Three Sisters Planting](img/02026-03-29-three-sisters-first-planting.png)

Because I had plenty of seeds, I decided to use the same seeds in a second planting
in the same bed in the Southwest corner. Here is the picture with the pH analog sensor:

![Second Three Sisters Planting](img/02026-03-29-three-sisters-second-planting.png)
